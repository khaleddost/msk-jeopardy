explanation: >
  In a child with elbow trauma, elevated **anterior (sail) and posterior fat
  pads** are highly suggestive of an occult **supracondylar fracture**, the most
  common pediatric elbow fracture. Confirm by checking that the **anterior
  humeral line** passes through the middle third of the capitellum.
