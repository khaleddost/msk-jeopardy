explanation: >
  A **toddler fracture** is a non-displaced **spiral fracture of the distal
  tibia** in young ambulating children. It is frequently **occult or very
  subtle** on initial radiographs, so a normal first film does not exclude it —
  follow-up or further imaging may be needed.
