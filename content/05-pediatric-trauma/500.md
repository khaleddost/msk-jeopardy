explanation: >
  Ossification centers appear in the order **CRITOE** (Capitellum, Radial head,
  Internal/medial epicondyle, Trochlea, Olecranon, External/lateral
  epicondyle). If a supposed "trochlea" is seen **before** the medial epicondyle
  has ossified, the displaced **medial epicondyle** has been avulsed and
  **entrapped within the joint** — a commonly missed pitfall.
