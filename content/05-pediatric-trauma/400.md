  **Classic metaphyseal lesions** (corner and bucket-handle fractures) are
  highly **specific for non-accidental trauma (NAT)**. Posterior rib fractures,
  scapular/sternal fractures, and **fractures of varying ages** should raise the
  same concern and prompt a skeletal survey.
