options:

  B: Nightstick fracture
  C: Galeazzi fracture
  D: Essex-Lopresti injury
explanation: >
  A **Galeazzi fracture** is a **distal radial shaft** fracture with disruption
  of the **distal radioulnar joint (DRUJ)**. Mnemonic **MUGR**: Monteggia =
  Ulna; Galeazzi = Radius. Distinguish from the **Essex-Lopresti** injury
  (radial head fracture with interosseous membrane disruption and DRUJ
  instability).
