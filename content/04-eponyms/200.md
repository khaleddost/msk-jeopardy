explanation: >
  A **Bennett fracture** is a **two-part** intra-articular fracture-dislocation
  at the **base of the first metacarpal**; the small volar (beak) fragment stays
  put while the shaft is pulled proximally by abductor pollicis longus. The
  comminuted (T-/Y-shaped) version is the **Rolando fracture**, which has a worse
  prognosis.
