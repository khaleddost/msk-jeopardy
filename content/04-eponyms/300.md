explanation: >
  A **Jones fracture** is a transverse fracture at the **metadiaphyseal
  junction** of the 5th metatarsal (within ~1.5 cm of the tuberosity). It sits
  in a **watershed** blood supply and has a high non-union rate — unlike the
  more proximal **pseudo-Jones** avulsion of the tuberosity, which generally
  heals well.
