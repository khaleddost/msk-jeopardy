options:
  A: Smith fracture
  B: Barton fracture
  C: Chauffeur (Hutchinson) fracture
  D: Die-punch fracture
explanation: >
  A **Barton fracture** is an **intra-articular** distal radius rim fracture
  (dorsal or volar) with associated **radiocarpal subluxation** — the
  subluxation is what sets it apart. A **Chauffeur (Hutchinson)** fracture is an
  isolated **radial styloid** fracture; a **die-punch** is a depressed fracture
  of the lunate fossa of the distal radius.
