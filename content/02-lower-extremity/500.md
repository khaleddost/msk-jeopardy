explanation: >
  A **Maisonneuve fracture** is a **proximal fibula** fracture associated with
  an unstable ankle injury (medial malleolus fracture or deltoid rupture with
  syndesmotic disruption). The ankle injury looks deceptively isolated, so
  always image the **entire fibula** — the syndesmotic instability usually
  requires surgical fixation.
