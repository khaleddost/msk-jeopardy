options:
  A: Weber A fracture
  B: Weber B fracture
  C: Weber C fracture
  D: Weber D fracture
explanation: >
  A **Weber B** fracture is a fibular fracture **at the level of the
  syndesmosis** (tibial plafond). The Weber A/B/C system classifies the fibular
  fracture by its level relative to the plafond and predicts syndesmotic
  stability.
