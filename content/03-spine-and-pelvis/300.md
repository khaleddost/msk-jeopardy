explanation: >
  Widening of the **pubic symphysis** (> 2.5 cm) indicates an **anteroposterior
  compression (open-book)** injury. The pelvis is a **ring**, so it almost never
  breaks in just one place — always search posteriorly for an **SI joint** or
  sacral disruption, which determines instability and bleeding risk.
